My name is Mac Hale and I currently live in Denver Colorado. I grew up in the small town of Whitewater, Wisconsin where I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Marketing with an art minor. When I turned 15 I began working at my family’s small lumber company Home Lumber. Working in the lumber yard over the years, it was challenging moving up in the company to Operations Forman while attending the University of Wisconsin Whitewater. Working and attending school helped build the foundation for a strong set of ethics and values which I apply on a daily basis.



Interests: Woodworking • Ceramics • Guitar • Motorcycles • Snowboarding



Realizing I don’t have a passion for marketing, I decided to attend the Art Institute of Colorado when I moved out here in Denver in 2016. There I found a new passion for graphic and web design and graduated with a diploma in Web Development. Even though I graduated I continue to harness and improve my skills with HTML/CSS and Javascript every day. Denver is my new home, web design is my new career path, and I am determined to achieve this path and do the best that I can.
