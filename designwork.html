    Design Work

    • "Harley Military Tribute"

    Description: This is an educational piece focusing on the elements of typography. I learned how to utilize many tools in Photoshop to create different aspects of this piece such as the neon sign, the text on the ground, the "HD" paint on the wall, and the gradient effect.

    • "Face Paint"

    Description: This is a Photoshop piece I created utilizing the eyedropper and brush tools to paint over an existing image. I learned how to properly utilize these tools by adjusting the brush hardness, size, and opacity to produce realistic effects of the image.

    • "Ski Trip"

    Description: This is an advertisement mock-up for Burton Snowboard Company that I created in Photoshop. I strategically placed the Burton logo throughout the photo and a slogan at the bottom to achieve a compelling ad.

    • "Tomehto or Tomahto?"

    Description: This piece was created from scratch in Illustrator predominantly focused on the pen, mesh, and eyedropper tools. It seems fairly basic yet it was challenging to produce the proper effects of the shape, lighting, and shading.

    • "Sherlock and His Sidekick"

    Description: This is a piece I created in Illustrator with a Sherlock Holmes theme. It consists of 16 seperate pieces assembled together to produce one large cohesive piece. The challenge was to create 16 small pieces strictly utilizing typography to create illustrations and following a specific theme. When it comes to my design work this was one of my favorite pieces to work on.

    • "Vitality"

    Description: I created this logo while interning at Denver Print Company for one of their clients whom operated a small health and wellness company called Vitality.
